Reimagining CTRL

CTRL Reimagined is an expansion of the promotional content for the album's release with a focus on the album's central theme of the relationship between technology, humanity, and authenticity.

  • In CTRL, SZA emphasizes the dichotomy of nature and technology to expose the inauthenticity of the digital age. The album reads as a confessional diary on the complexities of modern relationships.
